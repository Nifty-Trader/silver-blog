Silver remains one of the most popular investment options in India, especially in cities like Ahmedabad where people buy silver for jewellery, gifting, trading, and long-term savings. If you are planning to invest in silver, tracking the daily silver rate is very important because prices change regularly based on global and domestic market trends.
According to the latest updates, the silver rate in Ahmedabad today is approximately ₹2,650 to ₹2,700 per 10 grams, while 1 kilogram silver is trading around ₹2,65,000 to ₹2,70,000 depending on market movement and local jeweller charges. 
Silver prices in Ahmedabad mainly depend on international silver prices, MCX commodity trading, the US dollar index, industrial demand, and import duties in India. Gujarat is also known for active jewellery and bullion trading, which keeps silver demand strong throughout the year. 
Why Silver Investment is Popular in Ahmedabad
Many investors in Ahmedabad prefer silver because it is more affordable than gold and has strong industrial demand.

Silver is widely used in:

Jewellery and ornaments

Electronics and solar panels

Investment coins and bars

Religious and traditional items


Silver is considered a safer investment during inflation and uncertain market conditions. Beginners also prefer silver because they can start investing with smaller amounts.
Factors Affecting Silver Price in Ahmedabad
Several factors impact silver prices daily:


International market trends


Rupee vs US dollar movement


Import duty and GST


Demand during festivals and weddings


Industrial consumption


Currently, silver purchases in India attract 3% GST, which slightly affects the final retail price. 
Should You Buy Silver Now?
Experts believe silver may continue to remain important because of increasing industrial usage in renewable energy and electronics sectors. However, silver prices are volatile, so investors should track daily price movements before investing.
